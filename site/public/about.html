More than a Tapas Bar

Our love affair with Spanish food started over a decade ago when we lived in Melilla. We developed a border line Tapas obsession in the T and J taquerias. Unable to fuel our addiction back in the UK we quit our day jobs and set up T&J Tapas.

Our goal is to create the kind of Tapas restaurants we would like to visit if we weren't so busy running them. We're committed to making ridiculously good Tapas with fresh ingredients, from scratch every day. We hope you love what we do.

We pride ourselves on our craveable Iberian-Inspired menu, along with our standards on food safety and product quality. We have a maniacal focus on our customers, never follow, and always celebrate the accomplishments of our people. Sound like you?

Tharidu Jayaratne and James Lim.
